What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is an AI-powered software that can simulate a conversation with users in natural language through messaging applications, websites, mobile apps, or the telephone.

Benefits of Using Chatbots

Chatbots can provide 24/7 customer support, handle multiple queries simultaneously, improve customer engagement, and reduce operational costs.
